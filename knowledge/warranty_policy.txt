WARRANTY POLICY - Pinnacle Home Services

All work performed by Pinnacle Home Services comes with our satisfaction guarantee.

LABOR WARRANTY:
- All labor is covered by a 90-day warranty from the date of service completion.
- If the same issue reoccurs within 90 days, we will return and fix it at no additional labor cost.
- The warranty covers the specific repair performed, not unrelated issues.

PARTS WARRANTY:
- Parts installed by Pinnacle Home Services carry the manufacturer's warranty, which varies by product.
- Standard parts (faucets, valves, switches, outlets): typically 1-year manufacturer warranty.
- Major equipment (water heaters, AC units, furnaces): typically 5-10 year manufacturer warranty.
- We handle all warranty claims with the manufacturer on your behalf at no extra charge.

WHAT'S NOT COVERED:
- Damage caused by misuse, neglect, or unauthorized modifications after our service.
- Issues caused by pre-existing conditions not related to our repair.
- Normal wear and tear after the warranty period.
- Damage from natural disasters, power surges, or acts of God.

HOW TO FILE A WARRANTY CLAIM:
- Call us at (512) 555-0100 and reference your original confirmation number.
- A technician will be scheduled to assess the issue, typically within 1-2 business days.
- If the issue is covered under warranty, the repair will be completed at no charge.

SATISFACTION GUARANTEE:
- If you're not satisfied with our work, let us know within 30 days.
- We'll send a senior technician to review and resolve the issue.
- If we can't fix it to your satisfaction, we'll refund your labor cost.
