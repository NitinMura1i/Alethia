FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS - Pinnacle Home Services

Q: Do you offer free estimates?
A: We offer free phone estimates for standard services. For complex jobs that require an on-site assessment, there is a $49 diagnostic fee which is waived if you proceed with the repair.

Q: How quickly can you get a technician out?
A: For routine appointments, we typically schedule within 1-3 business days. For urgent issues, we offer same-day or next-day service when available. For emergencies (burst pipes, gas leaks, electrical hazards, no heat/AC in extreme weather), we have 24/7 emergency service available.

Q: Do I need to be home during the appointment?
A: Yes, an adult (18+) must be present during the entire appointment. The technician needs to discuss findings with you and get approval before starting work.

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?
A: We accept cash, personal checks, and all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover). Payment is due upon completion of service.

Q: Do you offer financing?
A: Yes, we offer financing options for jobs over $1,000 through our partner GreenSky. You can apply at the time of service, and approval takes just a few minutes. We offer 0% APR for 12 months on approved credit.

Q: Are your technicians licensed and insured?
A: Yes, all Pinnacle Home Services technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our plumbers hold Texas State Plumbing licenses, our electricians hold Texas Electrical licenses, and our HVAC technicians hold EPA Section 608 certifications.

Q: Do you charge extra for weekends or after-hours?
A: Saturday appointments during our regular Saturday hours (9am-2pm) are at standard rates. Emergency calls outside of business hours have a $99 after-hours surcharge in addition to the standard service cost.

Q: Can I request a specific technician?
A: Absolutely! If you've had a great experience with a specific technician, let us know when scheduling and we'll do our best to accommodate your request, though it may affect scheduling availability.

Q: What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
A: We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. There's no fee for rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a $25 cancellation fee.

Q: Do you pull permits for larger jobs?
A: Yes, for any work that requires a permit (panel upgrades, water heater installations, major plumbing work, etc.), we handle the entire permitting process. The cost of permits is included in our quote.

Q: Do you offer maintenance plans?
A: Yes! Our Pinnacle Protection Plan includes annual HVAC tune-ups, plumbing inspections, and electrical safety checks for $199/year. Members also get 15% off all repairs and priority scheduling.
